About Us

Engineering Precision,
Delivering Excellence

PrecisionWorks Manufacturing was founded with a singular mission: to produce components of uncompromising quality for the world's most demanding industries. From aerospace and automotive to medical devices and industrial automation, our parts are trusted where failure is not an option.

Our state-of-the-art facility houses advanced 5-axis CNC machining centers, wire EDM systems, and precision grinding equipment — all operated by skilled engineers who understand that every micron matters. We maintain ISO 9001:2015 certification and rigorous quality inspection at every stage.

With over 25 years of experience and 500+ satisfied global clients, we combine deep technical expertise with agile manufacturing capabilities to deliver precision-engineered solutions, on time and on spec.
